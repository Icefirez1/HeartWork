Featured Artwork! Be Perfect by Naomi Pagar

Naomi Pagar's painting has the great meaning. THe meaning behind this piece would be that the girl is being consumed by the expectations and standards around her that the world and society and even herself have put upon her; the need to be perfect and beautiful and have no flaws or blemishes. The inspiration for this piece directly correlates to teh pressures of perfection Naomi has felt in her own life and that young teen girls in general feel by the expectations that society and social media press on us.
